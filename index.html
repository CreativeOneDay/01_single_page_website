Why you should care

Here is the thing.

Your ideation muscle (aka your brain) just works like any other muscle.

If you train your capacities for idea generation, you will be able to come up with plenty of ideas in any given situation. If not, it starts to athropy and you will find it hard to come up with ideas.

Become an idea athlete and you will never again ran out of interesting options, promise.
